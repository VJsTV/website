**Moment Factory** is the gold standard of large-scale multimedia experience production — a Montréal-founded studio that has grown into a global operation with offices in Paris, Tokyo, Los Angeles, London, and New York.

Their physical studio infrastructure is purpose-built for the complexity of their commissions: massive "Black Box" spaces where full-scale replicas of stadium stages can be constructed to test and refine visual designs before a single piece of equipment hits a tour truck. This level of prototyping is what separates Moment Factory from smaller operations — they can verify that a 40-metre LED wall behaves exactly as planned in a controlled environment before it goes in front of 80,000 people.

Major commissions include visual production for **Madonna**, **Red Hot Chili Peppers**, and permanent installations at landmarks worldwide, including the first projection mapping on the Nativity façade of **La Sagrada Família**. In 2026, Moment Factory is among the studios engaged with the **Las Vegas Sphere** ecosystem — the most technically ambitious entertainment venue ever built.

Their "Lumina Night Walks" — permanent illuminated forest walk experiences installed in natural settings across North America and beyond — have become a distinct product category that the studio has virtually invented.
