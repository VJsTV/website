**Derivative** are the creators of **TouchDesigner** — the visual programming environment that has become the lingua franca of live AV performance, interactive installation, and projection mapping. Founded as a spin-off from Alias|Wavefront (creators of Maya), Derivative have spent over two decades developing a platform used by virtually every major studio in this field.
