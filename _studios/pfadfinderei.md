**Pfadfinderei** are a Berlin-based graphic design and visual studio whose flat, colour-saturated visual language has made them one of the most recognisable studios in European electronic music culture. Their long-term creative partnership with **Robyn** spans multiple album cycles, creating a coherent visual world as influential as the music itself. They have also created work for **Modeselektor**, **Ellen Allien**, and other leading Berlin artists.
