**Quayola** is a London-based Italian artist whose practice investigates the boundaries between the real and the artificial, the classical and the computational. His live AV performance practice creates real-time generative environments responding to musical performance with extraordinary visual intelligence.

Quayola's work has been exhibited at the **Barbican**, **Centre Pompidou**, **V&A**, **Grand Palais**, and major institutions worldwide.
