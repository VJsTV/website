**Playmodes** are a Barcelona-based audiovisual art studio founded by Salva Sanchis and Marc ViladeFeliu, approaching each project as an integrated audiovisual experience. The studio have performed at **Sónar**, **Mutek**, **CTM Festival**, and exhibited installation work internationally.
