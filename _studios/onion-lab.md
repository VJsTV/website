**Onion Lab** are a Barcelona-based digital art and projection studio creating immersive environments and architectural projections. Founded by José Luis de Vicente and Lluís Nacenta, the studio bring digital technology expertise and strong art-historical knowledge to projects including **Nit de Museus** and **Llum BCN**.
