**Geometrica Studio** is a Madrid-based high-end visual label specialising in the intersection of motion design, projection mapping, and live visual performance — their name reflecting a defining aesthetic of geometric precision, spatial logic, and kinetic clarity.

In works like *NEXUS* and their broader live performance output, Geometrica treat architectural surfaces as instruments to be performed. The studio has produced work for major international events, festivals, and brand clients across Europe and Latin America.
