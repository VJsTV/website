**Bart Kresa Studio** are among the most technically ambitious projection design studios in the world, with a body of work that spans iconic buildings, the Las Vegas Strip, Burning Man, and international commissions from Asia to Europe.

Kresa himself is one of the most celebrated projection designers in the industry. Notable works include permanent and recurring installations on the **Griffith Observatory** in Los Angeles, major commissions at **Coachella**, and international projects across Asia and the Middle East.
