**Tundra** is an international collective that consistently redefines what a "screen" can be — working with grass, water, fog, and thousands of suspended light points rather than conventional display technology.

Their touring work *The Day We Left Field* features an inverted meadow of thousands of blades of artificial grass, each acting as a physical pixel in a massive, glowing, ever-changing image. Tundra are the collective festivals call when they want a genuine "world first."
