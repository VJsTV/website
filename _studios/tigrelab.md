**Tigrelab** is a Barcelona-based creative studio whose work ranges from broadcast identity design and motion graphics to projection mapping and live audiovisual performance, founded in the late 2000s.

Their commission for La Marató de TV3 — Spain's major annual charity broadcast — combined video mapping with vertical dance performance, creating a visually spectacular sequence that merged the physical and projected in a live broadcast context.
