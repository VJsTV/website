**nonotak** are a Paris-based light and sound art studio founded by Takami Nakamoto and Noemi Schipfer. Working with stroboscopic lighting, mirror systems, and spatial audio, the studio creates environments that operate directly on the viewer's perceptual apparatus — generating visual phenomena that are simultaneously physical reality and perceptual illusion.
