**Daito Manabe** is one of the most radical figures in contemporary media art — a Tokyo-based artist, programmer, and DJ whose practice consistently operates at the outer edge of what technology-mediated performance can do. As co-founder of **Rhizomatiks**, he has helped build one of the world's most technically ambitious creative studios.

Manabe has created visual systems for **Perfume**, Japan's most technically sophisticated pop group. He has exhibited at **MoMA**, **Ars Electronica**, **SXSW**, and major venues worldwide.
