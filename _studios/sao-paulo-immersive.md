São Paulo Immersive Lab is the premier fulldome production facility in South America. Located in the cultural hub of Vila Madalena, the lab houses a 10-metre inflatable dome with 6-projector seamless fulldome projection and a 48-channel spatial audio system.

The studio works exclusively with artists who push the boundaries of immersive storytelling, producing experiences that have toured from São Paulo to Amsterdam to Seoul.
