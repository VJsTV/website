**seeper** is a London-based arts and technology collective producing some of the UK's most significant architectural projection mapping works since the mid-2000s.

Their *LightLine of Gotham* (2010) — a spectacular 3D projection on Frank Gehry's IAC Building in New York for the inaugural Vimeo Festival — was one of the first major building projections staged in New York City.

seeper's broader practice includes interactive public art commissions, live event production, music video direction, and gallery installations, working with major brands and institutions in the UK, USA, and internationally.
