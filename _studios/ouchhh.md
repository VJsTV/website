**Ouchhh Studio** is an Istanbul-founded creative-technology practice that has established itself as a global pioneer of what they term "AI Sci-Art" — the transformation of scientific and biological data into large-scale public art through machine intelligence.

Their concept of **Neuro-Design** is one of the most distinctive approaches in the field: EEG headsets worn by performers or collaborators provide brainwave data in real-time, which is fed directly into generative systems that drive the studio's visual output. The resulting imagery is simultaneously biological and computational — shaped by the actual electrical activity of a human nervous system rather than by a designer's intentional choices.

Data sources used in Ouchhh projects include Istanbul's real-time wind patterns, the complete audio archive of orchestral performances, NASA space observation datasets, and neuroscientific research data from university collaborations. The studio turns these invisible rivers of information into "Data Sculptures" — monumental animated installations that make the abstract concrete.

With offices in Istanbul, Berlin, and Los Angeles, Ouchhh operate globally with a roster of institutional and public art commissions that has made them one of the most recognised names in the contemporary data art world.
