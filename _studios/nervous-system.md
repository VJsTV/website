**Nervous System** are a Massachusetts-based generative art and design studio founded by Jessica Rosenkrantz and Jesse Louis-Rosenberg. Using custom software that simulates natural growth processes — cellular division, leaf venation, coral growth — they create jewellery, housewares, and art objects through 3D printing and digital fabrication. Their work has been collected by the **MoMA** and exhibited in design museums worldwide.
