**United Visual Artists** (UVA) are a London-based practice whose large-scale sculptural installations and live AV environments have redefined what light and data can do in space. Founded in 2003 creating visuals for **Massive Attack**, **Chemical Brothers**, and **U2**, the studio's permanent installation *Volume* transforms the V&A's John Madejski Garden into a responsive field of sound and light.
