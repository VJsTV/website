**ScanLab Projects** are a London-based studio that has built a unique practice around LiDAR scanning — transforming an engineering instrument into a medium for capturing space, atmosphere, and time. Their works include LiDAR scans of the Antarctic, the Chernobyl exclusion zone, and historic London buildings.
