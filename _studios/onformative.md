**onformative** is a Berlin-based studio that operates at the intersection of generative design and commercial production — creating visual systems for brands, institutions, and exhibitions that behave like living organisms rather than designed objects.

Their signature approach treats visual systems as dynamic, rule-governed processes rather than static compositions. A logo animation for Audi, an installation for Nike, a data visualisation for a cultural institution — all emerge from the same generative logic: parameters set, systems released, beauty emerging from the interaction of rules and randomness.

This process-oriented approach connects onformative to the broader tradition of computational art and design pioneered by figures like Vera Molnár and John Maeda, while operating entirely within contemporary digital production contexts. Their work is simultaneously intellectually rigorous (the code is real, the systems are real) and commercially effective — a combination that has made them one of Berlin's most successful creative studios.

onformative have worked with brands including **Audi**, **Nike**, and major cultural institutions, and exhibit their independent practice at festivals and galleries internationally.
