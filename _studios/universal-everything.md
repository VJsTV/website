**Universal Everything** are a Sheffield-based digital art studio founded by Matt Pyke in 2004. Their *Future Self* series — animations of a walking human figure constructed from an ever-changing variety of materials and textures — has become one of the most celebrated bodies of work in digital art. They have created work for **Kanye West**, **Nike**, **Mercedes-Benz**, and **the Tate**.
