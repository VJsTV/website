**Stereolux** is a Nantes-based arts centre that is simultaneously a presenting venue for international artists, a commission and residency programme, and a production house. Based in the Les Machines de l'Île complex on the Île de Nantes, the venue has been the site of production and premiere for major works by leading international artists.
