**Rencontres Audiovisuelles** is a Lille-based organisation whose influence on global architectural projection mapping extends far beyond their productions. As organisers of the **Video Mapping Festival** — one of the world's leading events dedicated to architectural projection — they are simultaneously practitioners, curators, educators, and gatekeepers.

The Video Mapping Festival attracts leading mapping studios and artists from across Europe, the Americas, and Asia. The organisation also runs residency and talent development programmes, workshops, and international cultural exchanges.
