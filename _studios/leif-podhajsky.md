**Leif Podhajsky** is a London-based Australian visual artist whose distinctive psychedelic visual language has made him one of the most recognisable forces in contemporary music artwork. His album covers for **Tame Impala**, **Bonobo**, **Darkside**, and **MGMT** have contributed as much to visual culture as the recordings themselves.

Beyond album artwork, Podhajsky creates live visual productions and video installations that translate his aesthetic into temporal and spatial experiences.
