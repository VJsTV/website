**Silasveta** (which translates roughly as 'force of light') are a St. Petersburg-based projection mapping and light art studio whose work has brought international recognition to Russia's creative technology scene.

Founded in 2009, Silasveta have developed a practice built on architectural projection at the largest scales — transforming historic buildings, public squares, and festival environments into luminous canvases that combine technical precision with strong visual storytelling.

The studio have created projections for the **White Nights Festival** in St. Petersburg, **Nuit Blanche** in Paris, and major light festivals across Europe and Asia. Their work is characterised by a rigorous attention to the specific architectural character of each building they illuminate.
