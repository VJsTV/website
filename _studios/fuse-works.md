**Fuse Works** are a Cape Town-based digital art studio creating generative artworks and interactive installations for cultural institutions across Africa and internationally. Founded in 2008, they have established themselves as one of Africa's most internationally recognised media art studios, presenting at the **National Arts Festival**, **Design Indaba**, and exhibitions in Europe and Asia.
