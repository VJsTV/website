**404.zero** is a nomadic creative operation whose defining philosophical commitment is the "No-Loop" principle: every visual element in every performance or installation is generated live, in real-time, from mathematical processes. Nothing is pre-rendered; nothing is played back. If a system crashes, the piece ends.

This radical commitment to liveness produces work of distinctive intensity. Operating from a "studio" that is often a dark room filled with modular synthesisers, custom-coded **TouchDesigner** patches, and a forensic attention to mathematical precision, 404.zero create installations that function like controlled demolitions of visual space — strobe-heavy, high-contrast, and physically confrontational.

Their practice — moving between St. Petersburg and Los Angeles — sits firmly in the tradition of artists for whom generative process is not a tool but an ethics: a commitment to the idea that real art cannot be recorded, archived, or replayed, only experienced in the moment of its creation.

The studio's work has been presented at underground events, experimental festivals, and installation contexts where audiences prepared for genuinely challenging sensory experience.
