**m+plus** are a Seoul-based interactive media studio creating large-scale LED installations and projection mapping for public spaces and cultural institutions across South Korea and Asia. Founded in 2010, they have built a reputation demonstrating that Seoul's creative technology scene operates at the same level as studios in London, Berlin, and New York.
