**Silent Partners Studio** are responsible for some of the most-watched visual productions in the world — the visual direction behind tours and broadcasts for **Taylor Swift**, **Beyoncé**, **SZA**, and other artists performing at the absolute summit of the contemporary music industry.

Their specialism is **Extended Reality (XR)**: the integration of physical LED stage elements with real-time virtual backgrounds rendered in game engines and composited live for broadcast. Where previous generations of concert production used physical sets, XR allows performers to exist within fully volumetric digital worlds — environments that can change in real-time, that respond to the performance, and that look, in broadcast, indistinguishable from physical reality.

The studio operates between Montréal and Los Angeles and maintains relationships with the world's leading event directors, choreographers, and music industry creative directors. Their technical infrastructure — LED volumes, virtual production camera systems, real-time rendering pipelines — represents the current state of the art in live visual production.

In 2026, Silent Partners Studio are one of the most sought-after visual production partners in the industry.
