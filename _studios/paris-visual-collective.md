Paris Visual Collective is less a traditional studio and more a living organism. Founded in 2018, the collective hosts monthly residencies where artists share tools, techniques, and code — creating a collaborative culture rare in the highly competitive visual performance world.

The Belleville space has produced landmark works that have gone on to premiere at Centre Pompidou, Nuit Blanche, and internationally at Ars Electronica.
