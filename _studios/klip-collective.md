**Klip Collective** are a Philadelphia-based projection mapping studio whose work spans cultural institutions, public spaces, and live events across North America. Founded by Ricardo Rivera, the studio approaches architectural projection as a form of site-responsive storytelling.

Their portfolio includes landmark projects at **Longwood Gardens**, **Franklin Square**, **The Philadelphia Museum of Art**, and major outdoor festivals. Klip Collective have also created projection works for **Universal Studios** and **Disney** properties.

The studio are notable for their integration of local cultural narratives into their projection content — researching each site's history and designing imagery that extends and illuminates it through light.
