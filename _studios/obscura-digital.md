**Obscura Digital** are a San Francisco-based studio whose 2015 projection of endangered species on the **Empire State Building** was seen live by 30,000 people and reached hundreds of millions globally. They have created projections for the **Vatican**, the **United Nations General Assembly**, and dozens of landmark buildings worldwide.
