**Joanie Lemercier** is a visual artist whose work with light and projection strips away theatrical spectacle, replacing it with a rigorous investigation of geometry, perception, and the fundamental physics of light.

Working with remarkably minimal hardware, Lemercier creates installations that transform how viewers experience space. Lemercier has also been a vocal critic of the environmental impact of digital art, publicly calculating the carbon footprint of his own NFT sales in 2021 and cancelling planned releases as a result.
