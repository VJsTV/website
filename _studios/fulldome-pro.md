**Fulldome.pro** is the global leader in the infrastructure of immersive dome experience — providing the physical structures, technical systems, and software frameworks that make 360-degree immersive performance possible at professional scale.

Where most studios focus on content, Fulldome.pro focus on the medium itself: the engineering of dome structures that can be deployed at festivals and events worldwide, the development of media server systems that can handle the extreme data demands of 360-degree projection without resolution loss, and the creation of tools that allow VJs and AV artists to perform within spherical environments with the same fluency they would on a conventional screen.

This infrastructure role is essential but often invisible — Fulldome.pro's domes and servers are inside countless festival and event productions without appearing in the credits. Their contribution to the expansion of dome VJing from planetarium shows into serious live performance contexts has been foundational.

The company's global network of dome installations and partnerships makes them the essential partner for any festival or institution seeking to build immersive dome programming.
