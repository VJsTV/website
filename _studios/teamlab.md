**teamLab** are a Tokyo-based interdisciplinary art collective whose immersive digital art installations have fundamentally changed how the world understands digital art. Their permanent venues — **teamLab Borderless** (Azabudai Hills, Tokyo), **teamLab Planet** (Toyosu, Tokyo), and international venues in Singapore and Shanghai — attract millions of visitors annually.
