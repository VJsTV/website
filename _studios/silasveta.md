**SilaSveta** (Сила Света, meaning "Power of Light") is one of Moscow's most celebrated creative studios working with projection mapping and immersive light art.

*KOLOBOK* — their interactive retelling of a classic Slavic folk tale — uses gesture-recognition technology to invite festival visitors to shape the projection's narrative in real time. SilaSveta have produced work for the Moscow International Festival of Light (Circle of Light) and major Russian and European brands.
