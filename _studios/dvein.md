**DVEIN** are a Barcelona-based motion graphics studio founded by Ferran Rossell and Teo Guillem. Their signature imagery — cells dividing, organisms growing, mechanical systems flowering into organic complexity — is produced through macro photography, CGI, and custom particle systems. They have created title sequences for **HBO** and **BBC**.
