**NOHLAB** is an Istanbul-based creative studio that has become one of the leading international voices in site-specific immersive installation. Their work is defined by a sensitivity to architectural context: rather than imposing digital content onto a space, NOHLAB create works in which the digital and architectural become genuinely inseparable.

This approach — which they call "architectural poetry" — has produced immersive exhibitions in some of the world's most significant historic spaces. Their work respects and amplifies the character of its environments while fundamentally transforming them through light, projection, and spatial sound.

NOHLAB have been commissioned by major cultural institutions and festivals across Turkey, Europe, and the Middle East, and have received international recognition for their contribution to immersive art and architectural media. Their work spans gallery installations, permanent public commissions, and large-scale festival spectaculars — all united by the studio's distinctive sensitivity to place and material.

Based in Istanbul, they represent the growing significance of Turkish creative studios in the global audiovisual arts landscape.
