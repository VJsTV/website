**Dumb Type** are a Kyoto-based interdisciplinary collective founded at Kyoto City University of Arts in 1984. Their work — spanning performance, installation, and video — positions them as one of the most important groups in the history of Japanese contemporary art. Key works include *pH* (1990), *S/N* (1994), and their 2022 **Venice Biennale** Japanese Pavilion project.
