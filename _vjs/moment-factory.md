**Moment Factory** is one of the world's most prolific and celebrated studios in the field of immersive multimedia experiences. Founded in Montréal in 2001, the studio has grown into a global practice with offices in Los Angeles, London, New York, Tokyo, and Paris, delivering landmark projects on every continent.

Their portfolio includes some of the most celebrated architectural projection mapping events of the modern era — from the first-ever mapping on the Nativity façade of **La Sagrada Família** in Barcelona (*Ode à la Vie*, 2012) to the largest immersive multimedia installation in any airport in the Americas (LAX Tom Bradley International Terminal, 2013).

Moment Factory work across a remarkable breadth of contexts: permanent installations in public spaces and museums; live concert visual production for artists including Madonna, Nine Inch Nails, and Arcade Fire; theme park experiences; urban light festivals; and branded events for major global companies. Their signature is a seamless fusion of technical mastery and narrative intelligence.
