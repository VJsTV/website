**30xN** is a Portuguese audiovisual artist whose work has earned them a place among the most distinctive visual voices in the European techno underground. Their visual language is spare to the point of near-absence: thin lines of light cutting through smoke-filled warehouse spaces, geometric divisions of dark rooms, the architecture of the venue made suddenly visible and intentional.

This is minimalism in its most rigorous sense — not a default absence of content, but a deliberate use of very little to achieve very much. A single vertical line of light in a dark warehouse, perfectly timed to a kick drum, can be more impactful than a thousand layered textures. 30xN understands this and builds performances around it.

Selected for the **25AV Residency Programme** for 2026–2027, 30xN is performing across Europe and developing new work that continues to push the boundaries of minimal audiovisual performance. They represent Portugal's increasingly prominent position in the European experimental electronic scene.
