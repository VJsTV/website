**Stef Mendesidis** is one of the most rigorous practitioners working at the intersection of live electronic music and live visual performance. As both a performer and producer — known for his four-deck, hardware-driven techno sets — Mendesidis brings the same precision to his visual work that he brings to his music: nothing is approximate, nothing is decorative, everything serves the whole.

The concept of the "Visual Wall of Sound" describes his approach: visuals and audio so tightly integrated, and of such similar density and physicality, that they merge in the listener's perception into a single sensory object. It is not music with visuals added. It is a unified audiovisual entity.

This level of integration requires extraordinary technical preparation: custom content precisely mapped to the audio's structural moments, SMPTE timecode synchronisation between the audio and visual systems, and a live sensibility developed over years of performance that allows spontaneous decisions to remain musically coherent.

Mendesidis represents the evolution of the producer-VJ — an artist who treats music and image with equal craft.
