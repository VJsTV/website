**Refik Anadol** is a Turkish-American artist, director, and producer known for creating monumental media art installations that blend architecture, technology, and artificial intelligence. His practice spans immersive installations, live performances, and architectural interventions that explore the convergence of technology, human perception, and consciousness.

Operating at an unprecedented scale, Anadol creates installations and experiences using machine learning, custom AI models, and real-time rendering systems that transform data into dynamic visual experiences. His works have transformed iconic architectural spaces worldwide — from Times Square to the Museum of Modern Art in New York, from the Metropolitan Museum of Art to major institutions globally.

His approach treats AI not as a technical tool but as a creative medium — using neural networks trained on vast datasets to generate visual poetry that explores themes of memory, consciousness, nature, and the human condition. The result is work that is simultaneously cutting-edge technically and philosophically profound, engaging audiences at the scale of cultural landmarks and moments.

Refik Anadol Studio operates internationally with a multidisciplinary team of artists, engineers, architects, and researchers, creating work that continues to expand the possibilities of what immersive media art can achieve.
