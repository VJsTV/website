**Refo** is an Amsterdam-based VJ and audiovisual artist whose live performance practice treats the visual set as a form of live composition. Rather than triggering pre-made content to music, Refo builds visual environments in real-time using custom tools that respond to the sonic environment with the immediacy of musical improvisation.

Based in Amsterdam's active electronic music scene, Refo has developed a performance practice that is technically sophisticated and musically literate — capable of operating at the level of intimate club environments and large-scale festival stages alike.
