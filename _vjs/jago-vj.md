**Jago VJ** is a Madrid-based VJ and visual artist whose live performance practice has made him one of Spain's most respected and active figures in the visual music world. Building real-time abstract visual environments that respond with precision and musicality to electronic music, Jago brings a consistent quality of visual intelligence to every performance context.

His technical range spans modular visual synthesis, custom GLSL shaders, and audio-reactive processing — tools that he deploys not for their novelty but for the specific qualities of response and expression they enable. The result is a live visual practice that sounds like it looks: structured, precise, and capable of sudden beautiful unpredictability.

Jago VJ performs regularly at major festivals and clubs across Spain and Europe, developing an international profile while maintaining a strong connection to Madrid's active electronic music and visual arts scene.
