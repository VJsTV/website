**Jacob Rendell**, working as **universal-order**, is an audiovisual artist and VJ whose practice centres on the precise choreography of generative real-time visual systems. His work sits at the intersection of VJ performance and video art — disciplined enough to be architectural, fluid enough to feel alive.

Works like *DIVINE CREATION* — built in Max/MSP/Jitter and VDMX5 to Richie Hawtin's *Orange / Minus 2* — demonstrate his ability to construct visual systems that respond to electronic music with structural precision. The imagery is geometric and layered, unfolding according to rules set in advance but executed in the flux of real-time performance.

Rendell shares work through his Vimeo channel and Tumblr under the universal-order identity, and has gained a significant following in the VJ and motion design communities for the technical sophistication and visual coherence of his output.
