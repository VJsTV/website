**VJ Eletroiman** is a Rio de Janeiro-based VJ whose psychedelic abstract visual performances are a vivid presence in Brazil's electronic music and audiovisual culture. Creating visual environments of vivid colour and rhythmic precision, Eletroiman brings the specific energy of Brazilian electronic music culture to live visual performance — a sensibility that is simultaneously technically accomplished and joyfully physical.

Active across Brazil and internationally, VJ Eletroiman is a central figure in Latin American VJ culture and a key presence at major Brazilian electronic music events.
