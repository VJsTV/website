**Jem the Misfit** is a Berlin-based VJ whose work inhabits the harder edges of the techno underground — creating visual environments of controlled disruption that match the relentless energy and physical intensity of hard techno and industrial music.

The aesthetic language of glitch — corrupted video signals, data errors, compression artefacts rendered visible — has a long history in experimental art, but Jem the Misfit brings it to live performance contexts with a precision and musicality that goes beyond simple shock effect. The visual systems she builds appear to be failing; they are in fact performing their failure with great accuracy.

Based in Berlin, Jem the Misfit performs regularly at the city's underground clubs and at hard techno events across Europe, developing a visual practice that is inseparable from the sonic culture she inhabits.
