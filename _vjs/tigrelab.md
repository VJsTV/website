**Tigrelab** is a Barcelona-based creative studio whose work ranges from broadcast identity design and motion graphics to projection mapping and live audiovisual performance. Founded in the late 2000s, the studio has built a reputation for visual work of exceptional clarity and dynamism.

Their commission for La Marató de TV3 — Spain's major annual charity broadcast — combined video mapping with vertical dance performance by **Sacude**, creating a visually spectacular sequence that merged the physical and projected in a live broadcast context. The project demonstrated Tigrelab's ability to operate at the intersection of performance, technology, and broadcast production.

Beyond live performance, Tigrelab have created motion design and identity work for leading broadcast organisations, cultural institutions, and brands across Spain and internationally. They are among the most consistently active Barcelona studios on the international festival circuit.
