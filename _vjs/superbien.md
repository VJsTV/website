**Superbien** is a Paris-based creative studio that has established itself as one of Europe's leading practices in architectural projection mapping and large-scale immersive experience design. The studio creates spectacular visual narratives for a client list that spans major global brands, cultural institutions, and live events.

Projects like *ORANGE — Show Hello!* (2016) demonstrate Superbien's ability to translate complex brand identities into extraordinary public spectacles — using architectural surfaces as canvases for kinetic light narratives that are simultaneously precise in their branding and expansive in their visual ambition.

The studio combines creative direction with in-house technical expertise across content creation, projection design, and event production — making them a full-service partner for the most ambitious immersive projects. Their work has been projected onto major landmarks across France and internationally, and the studio has won numerous awards for design excellence and technical innovation.
