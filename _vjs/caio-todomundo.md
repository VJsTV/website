**Caio Todomundo** is a Brazilian audiovisual artist and VJ whose work is characterised by vivid neon palettes, kinetic energy, and an exuberant visual imagination. His live sets — including *NINJA DRESSED IN NEON* — are celebrations of colour and motion that draw from street culture, digital art, and the tradition of Brazilian visual performance.

Brazil's live visual arts scene is among the most energetic in the world, with a community of VJs and audiovisual artists that reflects the country's deep tradition of combining music, movement, and visual expression. Caio Todomundo is a visible figure in that scene — performing at festivals and club events across South America and sharing work internationally.

His approach embraces the glitch, the loop, and the unexpected collision of visual elements — performances that are improvisational in spirit while remaining technically controlled.
