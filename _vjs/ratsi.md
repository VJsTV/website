**Ratsi** is a Paris-based VJ and generative visual artist whose work is defined by mathematical precision and a deep relationship with electronic music. Building visual environments from geometric primitives — grids, vectors, particles, fields — Ratsi constructs performances that evolve with the patience and intelligence of live composition rather than the frenetic energy of rapid-cut video.

His approach to the live visual set treats the performance space as a shared temporal environment between sound and image — one where the music is not merely accompanied but extended into a visual dimension. The precision of his generative systems means that every visual event is genuinely responsive to the music, not merely synchronised with it.

Ratsi performs regularly at leading European electronic music festivals and events, bringing a rigorous conceptual practice to contexts that more often prize spectacle over intelligence.
