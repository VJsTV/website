**Onionlab** is a Barcelona-based creative studio that has established itself as one of Spain's leading practices in projection mapping and large-scale immersive experience design. Founded in the late 2000s, the studio has grown from early experiments in architectural projection into a full-service creative agency working across art, culture, and brand sectors.

Their landmark work includes *A-cero XV Aniversario* (2011) — a spectacular 3D mapping that helped introduce the art form to Spanish and international audiences — and numerous commissions for festivals, landmarks, and cultural institutions across Europe, the Americas, and Asia.

Onionlab's approach combines strong conceptual intent with technical mastery, ensuring that their projects are as coherent in meaning as they are spectacular in execution. Based in Barcelona, they work globally and maintain strong connections to Spain's vibrant audiovisual arts scene.
