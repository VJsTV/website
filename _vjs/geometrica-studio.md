**Geometrica Studio** is a Madrid-based high-end visual label specialising in the intersection of motion design, projection mapping, and live visual performance. Their name reflects their defining aesthetic: a rigorous commitment to geometric precision, spatial logic, and kinetic clarity.

In works like *NEXUS* and their broader live performance output, Geometrica treat architectural surfaces as instruments to be performed — animating flat planes and volumes with choreographed sequences of light and pattern that feel simultaneously mathematical and intuitive.

The studio has produced work for major international events, festivals, and brand clients across Europe and Latin America. As makers of the *Making Of* film for the ARISTOCRAZY show at Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Madrid 2014, they have also documented the creative process behind large-scale visual production — contributing to the broader education of the audiovisual arts community.
