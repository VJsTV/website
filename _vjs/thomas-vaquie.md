**Thomas Vaquié** is a Paris-based VJ and visual artist whose audiovisual performances operate at the intersection of electronic music and abstract image-making. With a background that spans graphic design, programming, and musical performance, Vaquié brings an unusual breadth of perspective to the practice of live visual performance.

His performances are structured with the discipline of music composition — building visual systems that evolve across a set with genuine narrative intelligence, creating a temporal architecture that holds sustained attention without relying on the shock of novelty.
