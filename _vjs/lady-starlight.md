**Lady Starlight** — real name **Colleen Martin** — is a New York-based artist, DJ, and performer who occupies a unique position across mainstream and underground culture. Known internationally for her collaboration with **Lady Gaga** from the pair's early days performing together in New York, Starlight has maintained a parallel life as a rigorous underground practitioner whose "High-Voltage" performance sets push analogue hardware to its physical limits.

Her visual approach is radically analogue: where most contemporary VJs use software-driven systems, Starlight pulls visual signals directly from the voltage outputs of her synthesisers. The screen displays what the circuit produces — oscilloscope traces, voltage interference patterns, raw electrical signal made visible. No render engine, no GPU, no software. Pure electricity.

This approach produces visuals that have a rawness and immediacy that digital systems cannot replicate — because they are not replicas of anything. They are primary. The sound and the image are produced by the same voltage running through the same circuit; they are literally the same event in two sensory registers.

In 2026, Lady Starlight's underground sets remain a touchstone for artists interested in the physical reality of electronic performance.
