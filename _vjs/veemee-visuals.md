**VeeMee Visuals** is a Dutch collective operating at the very top of the festival VJ hierarchy. In a field full of talented practitioners, VeeMee have consistently set the benchmark for what high-end festival visual production looks like — combining studio-quality 3D content with the technical infrastructure required to deliver it flawlessly in front of tens of thousands of people.

Their signature is an aesthetic that bridges high fashion and high-energy performance: polished 3D renders that would be at home in a luxury brand campaign, pushed to the edge by the raw electricity of a festival mainstage. The result is visual production that is simultaneously premium and physically intense.

VeeMee manage ongoing visual relationships with dozens of the world's top 100 DJs, creating bespoke content and handling full stage visual design for arena and festival tours. Their infrastructure runs on Pixera and Notch, synchronised via SMPTE timecode to create airtight integration between every visual element on stage.

Among industry peers, VeeMee are the name cited most often when asked who sets the standard.
