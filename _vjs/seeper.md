**seeper** is a London-based arts and technology collective that has produced some of the UK's most significant architectural projection mapping works since the mid-2000s. Working across projection, interactive installation, and digital performance, seeper consistently positions itself at the edge of what technology enables and what culture demands.

Their *LightLine of Gotham* (2010) — a spectacular 3D projection on Frank Gehry's IAC Building in New York, created for the inaugural Vimeo Festival — was one of the first major building projections staged in New York City and helped introduce architectural projection to new American audiences at a defining moment for the art form.

seeper's broader practice includes interactive public art commissions, live event production, music video direction, and gallery installations. They have worked with major brands, institutions, and cultural organisations in the UK, USA, and internationally.
