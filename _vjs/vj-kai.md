VJ Kai is a pioneering visual artist based in Berlin, known for blending artificial intelligence with traditional VJ techniques. Their work explores the intersection of generative art and live performance, creating immersive visual experiences that respond in real-time to music and audience interaction.

With over a decade of experience in the audiovisual scene, Kai has performed at major festivals and clubs across Europe, including Berghain, Sonar, and MUTEK.
