**Jascha Süss** occupies a unique position in the VJ world — simultaneously one of its most technically sophisticated performers and its most important educator. His YouTube tutorial series and community resources have become the primary reference point for thousands of practitioners worldwide, making him a figure whose influence extends far beyond his own performances.

His performance setup is legendary in the community: a custom TouchDesigner interface that allows complex generative art to be played with the physicality and speed of competitive gaming. Using Xbox controllers, MIDI consoles, and the **OXI E16**, Süss performs what he calls "Visual Finger-Drumming" — real-time generative improvisation at a tempo and precision that feels impossible.

In 2026, he has been at the forefront of integrating **StreamDiffusion** into live performance — using AI to re-style live camera feeds in real-time at 60fps, transforming footage of the crowd, the DJ, and the venue into moving images in any visual style imaginable. The technology is new; the musicality with which he uses it is entirely his own.

Süss bridges the gap between underground performance practice and accessible education, making him perhaps the most important ambassador the VJ community has.
