**Novi_sad** is an experimentalist whose practice operates at the extreme edge of what audiovisual performance can mean. For Novi_sad, sound and image are not parallel or even complementary — they are the same thing expressed through different sensory channels, and the goal of performance is to collapse that distinction entirely.

His concept of "Sub-Bass Visuals" is the most direct expression of this: visual systems that respond not to the frequencies you hear but to those you feel — the sub-bass range below 80Hz that is experienced physically in the body more than audibly in the ear. The screen vibrates, smears, and distorts in response to physical vibration that the audience feels before they consciously register it.

The result is performance that operates simultaneously on conscious and pre-conscious sensory levels — you see something happening on screen that corresponds to something you are experiencing in your body, creating a unity of perception that conventional AV performance rarely achieves.

Novi_sad's performances are demanding and deliberately uncomfortable. They are not designed for casual entertainment but for an audience willing to surrender to a total sensory environment.
