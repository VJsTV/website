**VJAuX** is one of the most distinct visual voices in contemporary club culture — a specialist in the aesthetic of maximalism, combining the energy of 90s Eurodance with a retro-internet sensibility that feels both historical and completely now.

Where much contemporary club VJing has moved toward minimalism and brutalism, VJAuX goes the opposite direction: more colour, more motion, more imagery, more everything. Their sets are deliberately overwhelming in the best possible sense — a high-speed visual journey through early internet culture, cheesy 3D renders, Windows 95 interfaces, and bouncing geometries that have been lovingly elevated into something genuinely compelling.

The aesthetic connects to a broader cultural moment in which a generation weaned on broadband nostalgia is revisiting the visual language of the early web with fresh eyes and powerful hardware. VJAuX is at the front of this wave.
