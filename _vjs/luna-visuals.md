Luna Visuals is a Tokyo-based collective renowned for their breathtaking projection mapping installations. Their work transforms architectural spaces into living canvases, merging centuries of Japanese visual tradition with cutting-edge digital technology.

From ancient temples to modern skyscrapers, Luna Visuals has redefined how we experience built environments through light and motion.
