**COHERENT STATES** by **Urban Screen** — COHERENT STATES — a video mapping installation spanning 120 metres in length and 15 metres in height across a historic graduation works. Premiered at the Lichtsicht Biennale in October 2011, the work transforms the enormous timber structure into a shifting field of coherent light patterns derived from the architecture's own constructional logic.
