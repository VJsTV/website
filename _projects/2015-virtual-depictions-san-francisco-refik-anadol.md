**Virtual Depictions: San Francisco** by **Refik Anadol** — Public art project (2015) transforming San Francisco building lobbies into parametric data sculptures. Architectural, cultural, and environmental data visualized as dynamic, cinematic projections—collaborating with Kilroy Realty and SOM Architects to reveal the city's hidden stories through generative media and large-scale immersive displays.
