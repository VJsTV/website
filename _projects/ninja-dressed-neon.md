**NINJA DRESSED IN NEON** is a live VJ set by Brazilian audiovisual artist **Caio Todomundo** — a burst of colour, motion, and visual energy that represents the vitality of Brazil's growing live visual arts scene in the mid-2010s.

Caio's work is built on layered aesthetics — neon palettes colliding with kinetic typography, glitch interruptions, and rapid-fire visual association. The set draws from a broad visual vocabulary that sits at the intersection of street culture, digital art, and audiovisual performance.

Brazil has produced a distinctively vibrant community of VJs and live visual artists, and Caio Todomundo has been a visible figure in that scene — performing at festivals and club events across South America and sharing his work internationally through Vimeo and social platforms.
