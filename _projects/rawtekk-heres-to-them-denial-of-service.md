RAWTEKK: Here's To Them (Remastered 2020) by denial.of.service — All visuals created by denial.of.service. This music video combines cyberpunk aesthetics, dark dystopian atmospheres, and sound-reactive generative visuals inspired by Delaunay triangulation, Lloyd's algorithm, and Voronoi diagrams. The work was featured in Ars Electronica 2017, Berlin Fashion Film Festival 2017, and the 57th Krakow Film Festival.
