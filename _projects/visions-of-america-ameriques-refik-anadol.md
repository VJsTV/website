Visions of America: Amériques / LA Phil by Refik Anadol — Led by conductor laureate Esa-Pekka Salonen and performed by the Los Angeles Philharmonic, this multimedia presentation features Refik Anadol’s site-specific architectural video installation. Using real-time algorithmic sound analysis, depth camera tracking, and generative visuals, the project transforms Walt Disney Concert Hall into an immersive visual and auditory experience. The visuals respond to the music and conductor’s movements, creating a dynamic interplay between architecture, sound, and light.
