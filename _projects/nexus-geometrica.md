**NEXUS** is a live visual performance and projection mapping work by **Geometrica Studio**, the Madrid-based high-end visual label known for their precisely choreographed, technically ambitious approach to audiovisual production.

In NEXUS, Geometrica treated architectural surfaces as instruments — surfaces that could be played, struck, and transformed in real time in response to music and narrative. The visual language is characteristic of the studio: tight geometry, kinetic precision, and a command of negative space that makes even static moments feel charged.

Geometrica Studio has produced work for international festivals, brand events, and broadcast clients across Europe and Latin America, establishing themselves as one of Spain's most accomplished studios in the intersection of architecture and visual performance.
