**A-cero XV Aniversario** is one of Onionlab's most celebrated early projection mapping commissions — a spectacular 3D mapping onto the façade of Spanish architecture studio **A-cero**'s building, created to celebrate their 15th anniversary in June 2011.

Barcelona-based **Onionlab** — one of Spain's pioneering studios in projection mapping and audiovisual performance — designed a content narrative that reflected A-cero's architectural DNA. The geometry of the building became a canvas for choreographed sequences of light, volume, and transformation, turning the physical structure into a living, breathing entity for the duration of the evening.

The project was widely shared online and helped establish both Onionlab and Spain's projection mapping scene on the international stage.
