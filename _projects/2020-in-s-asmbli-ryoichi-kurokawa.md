**in s.asmbli** by **Ryoichi Kurokawa** — 2020 VR 360 video | stereo sound (alternative rendering). Laser-scanned 3D data of architecture, ruins, and nature superimposed and reconstructed into dynamic, mind-bending layers—exploring human-nature relationships through algorithmic distortion and immersive spatial audiovisual design. Full duration 6'48"; variants include dome/Ambisonics installations.
