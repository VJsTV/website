Onryō by denial.of.service — Official music video combining industrial cybernetic aesthetics, reaction–diffusion systems, and sound-driven generative visuals. Created using 3DS Max, VRay, FumeFX, Processing, Max/Jitter, Ready, After Effects, PFTrack, and audio software including Ableton Live and NI FM8/Kontakt. The piece integrates motion reference, optical flow, and processed sequences inspired by Susi Sie’s 'Soundscapes'. Featured on The Creators Project / VICE and STASH #122.
