**re-assembli** by **Ryoichi Kurokawa** — Installation, 2022: 2ch video | 4.1ch sound | 16ch strobe light. Duration: 8'00" loop. Reassembled 3D-scanned data of architecture, ruins, and nature distorted into intense, layered audiovisual fragments—amplified by synchronized multi-channel strobes for perceptual overload and exploration of human-nature intersections in a looped immersive environment.
