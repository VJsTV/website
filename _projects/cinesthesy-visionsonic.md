**CINESTHESY 1.0** is a major live audiovisual performance work by French artist **SATI** (also known for the duo Sati & Yro). Performed in full at the **VISIONSONIC Festival** — one of France's leading audiovisual performance events — the piece represents a mature investigation into the total unification of sound and image.

The title synthesises *cinesth-* (from cinema, movement) and *-esthesy* (from synaesthesia — the crossing of sensory channels). SATI's work deliberately collapses the distinction between composer and visual artist; in performance, these roles merge entirely.

VISIONSONIC has been central to the development of live audiovisual performance culture in France and internationally, providing a platform for artists like SATI to push the form into new territory.
