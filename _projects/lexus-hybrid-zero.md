**LEXUS HYBRID ZERO — Zero Tunnel** is an immersive interactive installation created by Korean creative studio **Binder-a** for the **Korea International Motor Show**. Commissioned by Lexus as part of their Hybrid Zero campaign, the installation placed visitors inside a tunnel of responsive light and motion designed to communicate the excellence of Lexus hybrid technology.

As visitors moved through the Zero Tunnel, the environment reacted — light patterns shifted, frequencies changed, and the experience became personal. The installation translated the abstract promise of hybrid technology — efficiency, intelligence, seamless energy flow — into a direct sensory encounter.

Binder-a has been one of South Korea's leading studios in experiential design and interactive installation, creating work for major brands and cultural institutions that demonstrates the country's growing influence in the global audiovisual arts.
