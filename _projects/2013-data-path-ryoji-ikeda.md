**Ryoji Ikeda :: data.path** by **Ryoji Ikeda** — Documentation of the 2013–2014 installation *data.path* at Espacio Fundación Telefónica, Madrid. Raw data streams rendered as intense, synchronized visual and sonic patterns—binary code, rapid sequences, and perceptual overload in a large-scale immersive setup. A pinnacle of data-driven audiovisual art.
