**Sydney Opera House** by **Urban Screen** — Urban Screen's audiovisual staging of the Sydney Opera House — a poetic homage to the architecture and the constructional concept of Jørn Utzon. As Utzon described it: the shells are like clouds. Urban Screen's projection responds to this with a visual programme that honours the building's own internal spatial and structural logic across its iconic shell forms.
