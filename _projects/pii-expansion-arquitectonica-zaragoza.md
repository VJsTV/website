PII Expansión Arquitectónica - Zaragoza Luce 2025 by Néstor Lizalde — PII Expansión Arquitectónica — a site-specific intervention transforming the façade of the Colegio de Arquitectos de Aragón into a responsive organism that reacts to public presence. Using 55 light points across windows and balconies, the piece creates a synchronized light and sound choreography, integrating contemporary artistic practices with historic architecture to generate a multisensory urban experience.
