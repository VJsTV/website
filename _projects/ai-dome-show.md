The AI Dome Experience represents a new frontier in immersive visual art. Using custom-trained AI models within TouchDesigner, VJ Kai created a fully generative environment that responds to spatial audio in real-time.

The installation ran for three weeks at the Berlin Digital Art Festival, attracting over 15,000 visitors who experienced a unique, never-repeating visual journey inside a 20-meter geodesic dome.
