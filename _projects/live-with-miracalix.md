**Live with MiraCalix** is a live audiovisual concert by **Quayola** and **Natan Sinigaglia**, performed with music by the composer and electronic musician **Mira Calix**. These excerpts capture the collaboration's signature tension between computational precision and improvisational freedom.

Quayola's visual practice centres on translating classical art, music, and natural phenomena into hybrid experiences that exist between the analogue and digital. In Live with MiraCalix, his real-time generative visuals respond directly to Mira Calix's score — abstract computational forms evolving in perfect counterpoint to the music.

The work is part of Quayola's broader body of live performance research, which has taken him to major festivals and institutions worldwide, from the Barbican to Sonar and Mutek.
