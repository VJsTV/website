BREEDLE (Official Video) by denial.of.service — Official music video by Extrawelt, with all visuals created by denial.of.service. The project took four months of formula experimentation and over 1000 hours of combined rendering using open-source raytracers. The video explores cyber-ecology, abstract electronic music visualization, photoreal depth of field, and Mandelbrot-inspired fractals. Sibling video for the track 'THE INKLING' can be found at https://vimeo.com/76123151.
