**Griffith Observatory** by **Bart Kresa Studio** — Bart Kresa Studio's landmark projection mapping of the Griffith Observatory in Los Angeles — one of the city's most iconic landmarks transformed into a canvas for cosmic imagery, scientific data visualisation, and abstract animation sequences. A recurring and evolving commission that has established Kresa as a defining figure of Los Angeles's projection art scene.
