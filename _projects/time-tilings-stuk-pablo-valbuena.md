Time Tilings [STUK] by Pablo Valbuena — Time Tilings [STUK] — Four site-specific interventions by Pablo Valbuena for the Artefact Festival at STUK Kunstencentrum. The project explores architecture as a successive visual experience, where light and space create temporal patterns that unfold as people move through the environment. Inspired by Le Corbusier's Modulor, the work choreographs the perception of architectural space in time.
