**Particle** is a landmark live cinema performance by London-based audiovisual collective **D-Fuse**. Premiered at the On_Off Festival in São Paulo, Brazil, the work marked a new phase in D-Fuse's ongoing exploration of real-time cinema as a performative discipline.

D-Fuse — founded by Mike Faulkner — has been at the forefront of VJ culture and live visual performance since the mid-1990s. Their work spans music videos, installation, public art, and live performance, consistently pushing the language of the moving image into new contexts.

Particle draws from D-Fuse's archive of original footage and processed material, assembled in real-time and shaped by the energy of a live audience. The performance at On_Off introduced the work to Latin America's vibrant audiovisual community and was subsequently taken to festivals worldwide.
