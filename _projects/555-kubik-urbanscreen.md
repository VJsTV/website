**555 KUBIK** by **Urban Screen** — 555 KUBIK — "How it would be, if a house was dreaming." Urban Screen's defining projection mapping work on the Kunsthalle Hamburg. The visual language is derived directly from the building's architecture — theoretic conception and visual patterns emerge from the structural logic of the facade itself. One of the most cited projection mapping works ever made.
