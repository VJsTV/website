**Infinity Room - [TIEE]** by **Refik Anadol** — Infinity is an immersive environment project by Refik Anadol. Part of the Temporary Immersive Environment Experiments (TIEE) series, this installation uses projections and reflections to generate an endless, hallucinatory spatial experience—transforming rooms into perceptual infinities through data-driven visuals and sound.
