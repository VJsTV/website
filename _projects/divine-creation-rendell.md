**DIVINE CREATION** is a solo VJ work by **Jacob Rendell**, built around generative real-time imagery composed and performed live to **Richie Hawtin**'s track *Orange / Minus 2* (from the Minus label).

Created using **Max/MSP/Jitter 5**, **After Effects CC**, and **VDMX 5**, the work is typical of Rendell's approach — dense layers of precisely choreographed geometric motion, driven by both temporal and spectral triggers from the music. The result sits somewhere between VJ set and video art: disciplined enough to be structural, free enough to feel alive.

Released through Rendell's Tumblr and Vimeo presence under the *universal-order* moniker, the piece circulated widely in VJ and motion design communities on its release, becoming a reference for technically sophisticated live visual performance.
