Contour & Shape by denial.of.service — Official music video combining human-data captured via Kinect v1, generative glitch and datamosh techniques, and 3D rendering in 3DS Max with Krakatoa + VRay. Processing3.x, Brekel, After Effects, Trapcode Form, and Python/Ruby scripts were used for complex generative and glitch effects. Audio features Roland TR-808, SH-101, Oberheim DMX, NordLead IIX, AKAI S-950, Yamaha CS15, Doepfer A-100, and NI Kontakt/Reaktor/FM8.
