Light Temple Tokyo transformed the historic Senso-ji temple grounds into an ethereal digital landscape. Using precision projection mapping across multiple surfaces, Luna Visuals created a 30-minute visual narrative that honored traditional Japanese art forms while pushing them into the digital age.
