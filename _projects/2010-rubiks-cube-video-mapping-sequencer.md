**Rubik's Cube - Video Mapping Music Sequencer** by **VJ Fader** — Custom Processing software maps video onto a 3x3x3 Rubik's Cube, turning rotations and solves into musical triggers via Ableton Live. A creative fusion of physical object interaction, projection mapping, and live sequencing.
