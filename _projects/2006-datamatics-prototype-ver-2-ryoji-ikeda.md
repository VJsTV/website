**Ryoji Ikeda :: datamatics [prototype-ver.2.0], 2006-08** by **Ryoji Ikeda** — Directed by Ryoji Ikeda; concept, composition: Ryoji Ikeda. Early prototype from the datamatics series (2006–2008), rendering invisible data as intense, synchronized visual and sonic patterns. A landmark early exploration of data as artistic material, influencing the artist's entire data-driven oeuvre.
