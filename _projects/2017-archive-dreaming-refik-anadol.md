**Archive Dreaming** by **Refik Anadol** — 2017 installation at SALT Galata, Istanbul. Commissioned by SALT Research, machine learning explores 1.7+ million archival documents to uncover and visualize hidden connections in Ottoman/modern Turkish history—rendered as immersive, generative data dreams through projections and interactive navigation.
