MadMapper is the leading software for projection mapping and spatial LED control. It enables artists to map video content onto complex architectural surfaces, creating stunning visual transformations of physical spaces.

Used by Luna Visuals and many other projection mapping artists, MadMapper bridges the gap between digital content and physical architecture.
