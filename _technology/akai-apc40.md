The Akai APC40 (and APC40 MKII) is the definitive clip-launch controller for VJs using Resolume, VDMX, or any OSC/MIDI-enabled VJ tool. Its 5x8 clip grid, dedicated faders, and send controls translate directly into a tactile, intuitive visual performance interface.
