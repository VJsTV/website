GLOW Eindhoven 2026 (November 7–14) is the Netherlands' premier light art festival. Every year, VJ teams and visual artists transform Eindhoven's buildings, streets, and public spaces with large-scale projection mapping, LED art, and immersive light experiences — drawing hundreds of thousands of visitors across eight nights.
