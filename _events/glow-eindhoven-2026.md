## GLOW Eindhoven 2026

**November 7–14, 2026 — Eindhoven, Netherlands**

GLOW Eindhoven is the Netherlands' premier light art festival, transforming the city's streets, buildings, and public spaces into an open-air gallery of light. VJ teams and visual artists create building-sized projection mapping works and large-scale LED installations across eight nights.

### What to Expect

- **Building-scale projection mapping** on Eindhoven's architecture
- **LED art installations** along a curated walking route through the city
- **Interactive light works** responding to audience participation
- **200,000+ visitors** across eight nights of free outdoor programming
- **International artist selection** with commissions for large-scale works

### VJ Technology in Use

GLOW artists use **MadMapper** and **Resolume Arena** for architectural projection mapping, **NovaStar** LED controllers for large-format LED installations, and **TouchDesigner** for interactive pieces. Production support includes high-lumen **Barco** and **Panasonic** projection arrays.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

GLOW Eindhoven is one of the largest light festivals in Europe, providing VJ teams with the opportunity to work at architectural scale with professional production support. Eindhoven's status as a design and technology hub (home to Philips and the Design Academy) creates unique crossover opportunities between VJ art and industrial design.
