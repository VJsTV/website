## Tomorrowland Belgium 2026

**Last two weekends of July 2026 — Boom, Belgium**

Tomorrowland is the world's most iconic electronic music festival and arguably the most visually spectacular live event on the planet. The legendary Mainstage and its surrounding areas at De Schorre festival grounds represent the pinnacle of stage design and visual production.

### What to Expect

- **The legendary Mainstage** — the world's most elaborate stage construction with integrated LED, pyrotechnics, and projection
- **16+ stages** across two weekends, each with unique visual identities
- **400,000+ attendees** across the two-weekend format
- **Bespoke visual content** created for each stage by dedicated VJ and design teams

### VJ Technology in Use

Tomorrowland's production teams deploy the industry's most powerful tools: **Disguise** and **Hippotizer** media servers, **NovaStar** LED processors, multi-million-pixel **ROE Visual** LED walls, and custom **Resolume Arena** setups for real-time VJ content.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Tomorrowland sets the global benchmark for festival visual production. Its investment in stage design and visual content creates opportunities for VJs and visual designers to work on the most ambitious and technically demanding live visual projects in the world.
