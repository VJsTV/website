## Flux @ MUTEK Montreal 2025

**June 12, 2025 — 22:00 EDT — Montreal, Canada**

MUTEK Montreal brought together **Flux Collective** and **Luna Visuals** for a landmark audiovisual experience. For the first time, these two collectives performed simultaneously at MUTEK's main stage, their visuals interacting and responding to each other in real-time — a cross-continental collaboration that defined the future of VJ performance.

### Highlights

- **Dual VJ performance** — Flux Collective (São Paulo) and Luna Visuals (Tokyo) performing simultaneously
- **Real-time visual interaction** — both artists' visuals responded to each other on-stage
- **MUTEK main stage** — one of the most prestigious VJ performance slots globally
- **Cross-continental collaboration** — bridging Brazilian and Japanese visual art traditions
