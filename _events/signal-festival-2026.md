Signal Festival 2026 (October 15–18, Prague) is Central Europe's leading light art and VJ festival. Transforming Prague's historic architecture into canvases for projection mapping, LED installations, and live VJ performances, Signal attracts over 200,000 visitors across four nights of free outdoor programming.
