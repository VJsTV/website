## Signal Festival Prague 2026

**October 15–18, 2026 — Prague, Czech Republic**

Signal Festival transforms Prague's historic architecture into canvases for projection mapping, LED installations, and live VJ performances. Across four nights, the festival attracts over 200,000 visitors to free outdoor programming in one of Europe's most beautiful cities.

### What to Expect

- **Architectural projection mapping** on Prague's Gothic, Baroque, and Art Nouveau facades
- **LED installations** in public parks and squares
- **Live VJ performances** in clubs and indoor venues
- **Interactive light art** responding to audience movement and sound
- **Free outdoor programming** — one of Europe's most accessible light festivals

### VJ Technology in Use

Signal Festival artists deploy **MadMapper** and **Resolume Arena** for architectural mapping, **TouchDesigner** for interactive installations, and high-lumen projection arrays from **Barco** and **Christie** to illuminate Prague's historic buildings.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Signal Festival is Central Europe's leading light art and VJ festival. Its free, public format democratises access to cutting-edge visual art, while its technical infrastructure provides world-class production support for participating artists. The festival's success has inspired similar events across Eastern Europe.
