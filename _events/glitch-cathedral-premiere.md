## Glitch Cathedral World Premiere

**October 31, 2024 — 21:00 GMT — London, UK**

One night only. **Pixel Storm's** Glitch Cathedral transformed a 400-year-old East London building into a canvas for digital deconstruction — cathedral-scale glitch visuals that blurred the boundary between sacred architecture and digital chaos.

### Highlights

- **Cathedral-scale projection mapping** onto a 400-year-old building facade
- **Glitch aesthetics** — the building's Gothic architecture distorted and fragmented in real-time
- **One-night-only premiere** — an exclusive VJs TV documented event
- **Pixel Storm's signature style** — abstract motion graphics and disorienting visual fields at architectural scale
