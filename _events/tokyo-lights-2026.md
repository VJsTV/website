TOKYO LIGHTS 2026 is one of the world's premier projection mapping competitions. Screenings take place across two weekends (May 23–24 and May 30–31, with the finale on May 31) on the landmark Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building. International artists submit 1-minute projection mapped works onto this iconic architectural canvas.
