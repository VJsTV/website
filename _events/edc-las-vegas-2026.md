## EDC Las Vegas 2026

**May 15–17, 2026 — Las Vegas Motor Speedway, Nevada, USA**

Electric Daisy Carnival (EDC) Las Vegas is one of the world's most visually spectacular electronic music festivals. The kineticFIELD mainstage and its surrounding stages at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway feature some of the largest LED installations in the live events industry.

### What to Expect

- **kineticFIELD mainstage** — the world's most elaborate stage design with massive LED arrays
- **8+ stages** each requiring dedicated VJ and visual content
- **Art installations** and immersive experiences across the festival grounds
- **Dusk-till-dawn format** — visual artists perform through the night under the Nevada sky

### VJ Technology in Use

EDC's production teams deploy **Resolume Arena** and **Hippotizer** media servers with **NovaStar** LED processors driving panels from **ROE Visual** and **Absen**. Content is delivered in 4K across multi-million-pixel LED surfaces.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

EDC represents the pinnacle of festival visual production in North America. The sheer scale of its LED infrastructure creates opportunities for VJs and visual designers to work on some of the largest real-time visual canvases in the world.
