## Primavera Sound Barcelona 2026

**June 3–7, 2026 — Parc del Fòrum, Barcelona, Spain**

Primavera Sound is one of Europe's most prestigious music festivals, celebrated for its curatorial vision and production quality. Multiple stages at Barcelona's Parc del Fòrum require world-class visual content and VJ performances throughout the five-day event.

### What to Expect

- **Multiple LED-equipped stages** requiring dedicated visual content
- **Indoor and outdoor venues** with different visual requirements
- **Diverse musical programming** from indie to electronic — each demanding different visual approaches
- **One of Europe's largest audiences** — over 200,000 attendees across the week

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Primavera Sound's diverse programming creates opportunities for VJs across all visual styles — from abstract generative art accompanying experimental acts to high-energy LED content for electronic music stages. The festival's scale and prestige make it a key career milestone for visual performers.
