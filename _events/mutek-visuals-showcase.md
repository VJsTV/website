## MUTEK Visuals Showcase

**June 20, 2025 — 22:00 EST — Montreal, Canada**

VJs TV presented a curated evening of visual performances at MUTEK Montreal. **Luna Visuals** and **Nova Spectrum** explored the intersection of technology and visual art in one of the world's premier digital creativity festivals.

### Highlights

- **Curated VJs TV showcase** — handpicked visual performances for MUTEK's discerning audience
- **Luna Visuals** — projection mapping and immersive dome experiences from Tokyo
- **Nova Spectrum** — large-scale LED visual design from Los Angeles
- **AI-driven visuals** — both artists incorporated AI-generated content into their live sets
