## Ars Electronica 2026

**September 9–13, 2026 — Linz, Austria**

Ars Electronica is the world's most prestigious media art and digital culture festival. Since 1979, it has been the definitive gathering for artists, scientists, and technologists exploring the intersection of art, technology, and society. The 2026 edition continues this legacy across the Ars Electronica Center and venues throughout Linz.

### What to Expect

- **DEEP SPACE 8K** — Ars Electronica's signature presentation space enabling visuals at unprecedented 8K resolution across floor and wall projections
- **Prix Ars Electronica** — the world's most respected award in digital and media art
- **Exhibitions** spanning AI art, data visualisation, interactive installations, and immersive environments
- **Performances** integrating live visuals with experimental music and dance
- **Symposia** on art, technology, and the future of visual culture

### VJ Technology in Use

Ars Electronica showcases the bleeding edge: **Unreal Engine** for real-time rendering, **TouchDesigner** for interactive installations, **AI systems** using **Runway ML** and **Stable Diffusion**, and the Centre's own **DEEP SPACE 8K** laser projection system.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Ars Electronica is where VJ culture meets fine art and academic rigour. The Prix Ars Electronica's recognition of visual artists provides career-defining validation, while DEEP SPACE 8K offers a presentation format unmatched anywhere in the world. Essential for any visual artist serious about pushing boundaries.
