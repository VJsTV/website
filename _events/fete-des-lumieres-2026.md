Fête des Lumières Lyon 2026 (December 5–8) is one of the world's oldest and most prestigious light festivals, attracting over 2 million visitors across four nights. Lyon's entire city becomes a canvas — from historic facades to river bridges — for projection mapping artists, VJ teams, and light designers from across the globe.
