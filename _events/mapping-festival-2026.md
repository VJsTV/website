## Mapping Festival 2026

**May 7–17, 2026 — Geneva, Switzerland**

Mapping Festival returns to Geneva with its signature open-call format — no fixed theme, no boundaries. For 10 days, Geneva's clubs, galleries, and public spaces become laboratories for audiovisual experimentation.

### What to Expect

- **Real-time AV performances** in club settings and purpose-built venues
- **Hybrid art–science installations** exploring perception, data, and physical computing
- **Live VJ sets** alongside electronic music acts
- **Workshops and masterclasses** in TouchDesigner, Resolume, and generative coding
- **International open call** — artists submit proposals for performances and installations

### VJ Technology in Use

The festival embraces the full spectrum: **TouchDesigner** for generative works, **Resolume Arena** for live mixing, **Processing** and **openFrameworks** for custom creative coding, and experimental hardware setups.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Mapping Festival is one of Europe's most experimental and respected audiovisual festivals. Its refusal to impose themes encourages radical experimentation — from club-scale VJ performances to large-format immersive installations. A must-attend for serious AV artists.
