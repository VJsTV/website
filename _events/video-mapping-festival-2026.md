The Video Mapping Festival 2026 kicks off April 10–11 in Lille before touring the Hauts-de-France region through October. The 9th edition features international artist residencies, architectural façade mapping contests, and award ceremonies for both student and professional categories — making it one of Europe's most prestigious projection mapping events.
