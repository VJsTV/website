## Coachella Valley Music & Arts Festival 2026

**April 10–12 & April 17–19, 2026 — Indio, California, USA**

Coachella is one of the world's largest and most visually spectacular music festivals. Across two weekends at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California, Coachella's stages demand world-class LED walls, projection systems, and immersive art installations.

### What to Expect

- **Multi-stage visual production** — each stage features unique LED designs and real-time VJ content
- **Immersive art installations** scattered across the festival grounds
- **Antarctic dome** and other purpose-built visual experience spaces
- **High-profile collaborations** between visual designers and headline musicians

### VJ Technology in Use

Coachella's production teams rely on **Resolume Arena**, **Disguise media servers**, and **NovaStar LED controllers** to deliver seamless 4K/8K content across stages ranging from intimate tents to the massive Coachella Stage.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Coachella represents one of the most prestigious VJ and visual design opportunities globally. The festival's investment in visual production sets industry standards and provides unmatched exposure for visual artists working at the highest level.
