## Mo:Dem Festival 2026

**August 3–9, 2026 — France (exact venue TBA)**

Mo:Dem (Momento Demento) is an underground electronic music and audiovisual gathering unlike any other. Here, visuals aren't an afterthought — they are described as "the face, the heartbeat, and the dimension" of the experience. For six days, VJs and visual artists transform the natural landscape into an immersive AV environment.

### What to Expect

- **Continuous VJ performances** across multiple stages running 24 hours
- **Site-specific projection mapping** onto natural landscapes and festival structures
- **Immersive AV environments** blending nature with digital art
- **Underground electronic music** — psytrance, experimental, and ambient
- **International VJ community** gathering from across Europe and beyond

### VJ Technology in Use

Mo:Dem VJs rely on **Resolume Arena** and **TouchDesigner** for live mixing, with outdoor projection setups using high-lumen projectors mapped onto trees, rocks, and purpose-built structures.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Mo:Dem represents the purest expression of VJing as an integral art form within electronic music culture. Unlike festivals where visuals serve the music, Mo:Dem treats visual art as co-equal — making it a pilgrimage for VJs who believe in the transformative power of audiovisual experience.
