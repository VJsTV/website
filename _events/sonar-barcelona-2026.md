## Sónar Barcelona 2026

**June 18–20, 2026 — Fira Gran Via, Barcelona, Spain**

Sónar Barcelona is one of the world's most important platforms for VJs, digital artists, and AV performers. Since 1994, Sónar has set the global agenda for the intersection of electronic music and visual art, and the 2026 edition continues this legacy across the vast Fira Gran Via complex.

### What to Expect

- **Sónar by Day** — technology showcases, AI art demonstrations, and immersive installations at Fira Montjuïc
- **Sónar by Night** — the world's most cutting-edge VJs and visual artists perform alongside top electronic music acts at Fira Gran Via
- **Sónar+D** — the innovation and creativity congress exploring AI, XR, and future visual technologies
- **SonarLab** — experimental space for emerging AV artists

### VJ Technology in Use

Sónar's stages feature state-of-the-art **Resolume Arena** and **TouchDesigner** setups, **Disguise** media servers, and increasingly, **AI-driven visual systems** using **Runway ML** and **Stable Diffusion** for real-time content generation.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Sónar is arguably the single most important annual event for VJ culture globally. Its equal emphasis on visual and sonic art, combined with the Sónar+D technology congress, creates an unmatched environment for visual artists to showcase their work, discover new tools, and connect with the global AV community.
