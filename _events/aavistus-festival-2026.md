Aavistus Festival 2026 (October 1–4, Helsinki) is the biennial of audiovisual art and VJ culture in Finland. Under the theme "Attention, please!" — exploring noise, signal, perception, and the politics of attention — this artist-led, non-profit festival opens its call for VJ sets, projection mapping, media art, and experimental audiovisual works.
