## Sónar Istanbul 2026

**April 10–11, 2026 — Istanbul, Turkey**

Sónar expands its global footprint with Sónar Istanbul 2026, bringing the festival's signature blend of advanced music and cutting-edge technology to one of the world's most culturally vibrant cities.

### What to Expect

- **High-end AV productions** across multiple stages
- **Visual artists and VJs** performing alongside top electronic music acts
- **Technology showcases** featuring real-time rendering and AI visuals
- **Networking opportunities** bridging European and Middle Eastern creative communities

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Sónar's commitment to visual art as an equal partner to music makes every edition a landmark event for VJs. Istanbul's position at the crossroads of Europe and Asia creates unique opportunities for cultural exchange in the visual performance community.
