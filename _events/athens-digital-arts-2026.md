## Athens Digital Arts Festival (ADAF) 2026

**April 3–7, 2026 — Athens, Greece**

The Athens Digital Arts Festival (ADAF) is one of Southern Europe's most respected platforms for digital art, VJ performance, and audiovisual experimentation. Now in its 22nd year, ADAF 2026 brings together international artists, technologists, and creative coders across venues in central Athens.

### What to Expect

- **Immersive AV installations** across historic Athenian venues
- **Live VJ performances** with audio-reactive and generative visuals
- **Workshops and talks** covering creative coding, AI art, and real-time rendering
- **International open call** for digital artists and visual performers

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

ADAF has consistently championed the intersection of technology and visual art in the Mediterranean region, providing a launchpad for emerging VJ talent and experimental AV practices. The festival's focus on accessibility and education makes it a key networking event for the European digital arts community.
