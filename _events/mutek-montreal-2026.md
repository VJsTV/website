## MUTEK Montréal 2026 — Edition 27

**August 25–30, 2026 — Quartier des spectacles, Montréal, Canada**

MUTEK Montréal marks its 27th edition in 2026 with a programme that continues to set the global standard for audiovisual performance. The festival's deep commitment to live VJ/AV integration makes it the most important annual gathering for the serious audiovisual art community.

### First-Wave Artists Confirmed

- **Jeff Mills** — legendary Detroit techno pioneer with immersive visual collaborations
- **Rival Consoles** — electronic musician known for deeply integrated AV performances
- **Ben UFO** — one of electronic music's most respected selectors
- **Voices From The Lake** — immersive ambient techno AV project
- **Polygonia** — cutting-edge generative visual artist
- *More announcements to follow*

### What to Expect

- **A/Visions** — MUTEK's signature programme of fully integrated audiovisual performances
- **Nocturne** — large-scale outdoor audiovisual experiences in Quartier des spectacles
- **Expérience** — immersive dome and XR performances
- **MUTEK Forum** — professional development and networking for the AV community
- **Fulldome screenings** and 360° immersive cinema

### VJ Technology in Use

MUTEK performances feature **TouchDesigner**, **Resolume Arena**, **Notch**, and increasingly **AI-driven visual systems**. The festival's dome and immersive spaces use **fulldome rendering pipelines** and **multi-projector blending systems**.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

MUTEK is the cornerstone event of the global audiovisual performance community. Its A/Visions programme represents the highest level of integrated AV art, while the MUTEK Forum provides essential professional development. Previous VJs TV features include "Flux @ MUTEK Montreal 2025" and the "MUTEK Visuals Showcase" — both landmark moments in our coverage.
