## Burning Man 2026

**August 30 – September 7, 2026 — Black Rock Desert, Nevada, USA**

Burning Man is the world's most experimental gathering of art, community, and radical self-expression. For VJs and visual artists, the playa offers unmatched creative freedom — from projection-mapped art cars and camp structures to dome installations and interactive LED sculptures across the temporary city of Black Rock.

### What to Expect

- **Art car visuals** — mobile projection and LED installations on moving vehicles
- **Camp projection mapping** — transforming temporary structures into immersive visual environments
- **Dome installations** — fulldome projections in geodesic structures
- **Interactive LED sculptures** — responsive light art across the playa
- **The Man and Temple burns** — the most iconic visual spectacles in contemporary art

### VJ Technology in Use

Burning Man artists use **TouchDesigner** for interactive installations, **Resolume Arena** for camp VJ sets, **NovaStar** LED controllers for large-scale LED art, and solar-powered or generator-driven projection rigs with high-lumen **Barco** and **Epson** projectors.

### Why It Matters for VJ Culture

Burning Man is where VJ culture meets radical art. The festival's emphasis on participatory, immersive art creates a laboratory for experimental visual practices that push technology, creativity, and endurance to their limits. Many innovations in projection mapping and interactive visuals were first tested on the playa.
