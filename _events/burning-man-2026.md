Burning Man 2026 (August 30 – September 7) is the world's most experimental gathering of art and community. For VJs and visual artists, the playa offers unmatched creative freedom — from projection-mapped art cars and camp structures to dome installations and interactive LED sculptures across the temporary city of Black Rock.
