1. **NTS Influence (The Schedule):** Use a clean, bold grid for the "Upcoming Broadcasts" and "Live Now" indicators. This builds anticipation and makes the site feel like a 24/7 destination rather than a static link.
2. **Boiler Room Influence (The Archive):** Focus on the "VJ as the Performer." Large, high-impact cards for past sets should be the centerpiece of the "Explore" section.
3. **Red Bull TV Influence (The Experience):** Use "Liquid Glass" design elements—transparent overlays, blur effects, and high-octane transitions—to create a premium feel that attracts high-tier equipment sponsors.
